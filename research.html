Research & Innovation

Building knowledge for the entire global mental health movement

Our Research Philosophy

LATERAL is building more than a service delivery organization—we're creating a permanent research platform that generates insights for the entire global mental health field. Our research is:

  • Community-Centered: Research priorities are informed by the communities we serve, not imposed from outside
  • Ethically Grounded: All research respects local values, ensures informed consent, and prioritizes participant wellbeing
  • Practically Focused: We study what works in real-world settings, not just controlled environments
  • Openly Shared: Our findings benefit the entire global mental health community

Key Research Areas

Cultural Adaptation

How can evidence-based therapies be effectively adapted across diverse cultural contexts while maintaining therapeutic effectiveness?

Implementation Science

What factors enable successful scaling of lay therapy programs? How can we overcome common implementation barriers?

Therapeutic Mechanisms

What makes lay therapy effective? Which components are essential and which can be adapted or removed?

Technology Integration

How can digital tools enhance—not replace—human therapeutic relationships and improve outcomes?

Cost-Effectiveness

How can we maximize impact per dollar spent? What delivery models provide the best value for communities?

Training & Supervision

What training methods work best for lay therapists? How can we ensure quality at scale?

Our Approach to Data

LATERAL employs multimodal data collection to build a comprehensive understanding of mental health interventions:

Data Collection Methods

  • Standardized outcome assessments measuring symptom reduction and wellbeing
  • Process data capturing how therapy is delivered and received
  • Qualitative insights from participants and therapists
  • Community-level indicators of mental health and social cohesion

Analytical Approaches

We use advanced analytical tools as enhancements to human insight, not replacements for it. Our analyses help identify patterns, predict outcomes, and optimize interventions while always keeping human judgment and community wisdom at the center.

Ethical Framework

All LATERAL research adheres to the highest ethical standards:

Community Involvement

Communities are partners in research, not subjects of it. Local advisory boards guide our work.

Data Protection

We maintain strict data security and privacy protections, respecting both international standards and local norms.

Benefit Sharing

Research benefits flow back to participating communities through improved services and capacity building.

Transparency

Our methods, findings, and limitations are openly shared with communities and the scientific community.

Building Tomorrow's Solutions

Our vision extends beyond current interventions. We're working toward creating a "basket of therapies"—a diverse set of evidence-based, culturally-adapted interventions that communities worldwide can choose from based on their specific needs and contexts.

Through continuous research and innovation, we aim to develop new therapeutic approaches, improve existing ones, and build the knowledge base that will enable mental health care to reach everyone who needs it.
